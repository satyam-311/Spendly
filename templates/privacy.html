Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and enabling filters and search within your account.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app for you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We apply standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access controls to protect your information.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may use trusted infrastructure providers (such as hosting or database services) that process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to request deletion of your data, please reach out to us at support@spendly.app.

We will respond to all privacy-related requests within 7 business days.
